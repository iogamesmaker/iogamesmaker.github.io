# Mesopotamia
In the Netherlands some students have to do a finals project before they graduate high school.<br>
For our finals project, we made a little videogame!<br>
My friend did 99% of the art, I did the code bit. Raw C++, only external library is some Perlin noise library and SDL2. Rawdogged it.<br>
If you're interested in playing it, be warned, it's buggy and unbalanced. Gameplay is (in my opinion) pretty fun. It's on the harder side of things though, but once you get the hang of it it should keep you entertained for maybe 30 or 45 minutes.<br>
<br>The soundtrack is pure fire, created by Alexander Ahura for one of my favorite flash games of all times: Snail Bob 3. The composer was fine with us using it, but told me the rights belonged to Hunter Hamster. I mailed them, got no reply so fuck it, snail bob 3 soundtrack!
if you represent hunter hamster studios shoot me an email and i'll remove the music and replace it with some other cool shit, i assume it won't be a huge deal.
You can play it [in the browser](/projects/Mesopotamia/play), or [download it on the itch.io page](https://iogamesplayer.itch.io/Mesopotamia).<br><br>
